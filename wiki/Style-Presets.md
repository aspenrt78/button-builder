## Using Presets

1. Click **Presets** button in the header
2. Browse categories
3. Click any preset to apply
4. Customize further as needed

## Preset Tracking

When a preset is active:
- The preset name shows in the Editor header
- Fields from the preset show a **◆** indicator
- Modified fields show a **✎** indicator
- Click "reset" to restore preset defaults

## Creating Custom Styles

Start with a preset close to your vision, then modify:

1. Apply a preset
2. Adjust colors, sizes, effects
3. Copy the YAML for your custom style
4. Save as a template in Home Assistant for reuse
